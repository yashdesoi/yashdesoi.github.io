A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME
Hello! My name is Yash and I'm a web developer based in Mumbai, India. I graduated in 2020 with a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering. So how did I get into programming? Well, I started coding when I was 18 y/o as a part of my course in senior secondary. My first programming language was C++, did well in the course but after my senior secondary I pretty much never touched C++ again. Since I was good in Physics and Maths, I decided to go with Mechanical Engineering. But in my third year of engineering, I realized something was not right, I might be scoring enough marks in exams just for the sake of passing the semester, but I'm not really enjoying what I'm doing. So after months of thorough introspection, one fine day I found a video tutorial on Python by freeCodeCamp on YouTube. I decided to give it a try, and GOD! I fell in love with programming. Unlike C++, Python is more intuitive. After further exploration in this field, I came to know about Web Development, and later found out that JavaScript is the language of the Web. I joined n-number of courses to learn JavaScript, fell in love with it too. JavaScript and Python being higher-level languages keep the low-level abstraction away from us. After that, I made a decision to switch my career path and pursue a career Web Development and never looked back :)
